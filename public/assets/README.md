## How the Video Works:

1. A **Play button** appears on your profile photo
2. When visitors click it, a fullscreen modal opens
3. Your intro video plays automatically with controls
4. Visitors can close it anytime by clicking X or pressing ESC

## Video Recording Tips:

- Use good lighting (natural light or ring light)
- Clear audio (use a microphone if possible)
- Clean background (or use virtual background)
- Look at the camera
- Smile and be enthusiastic!
- Keep it professional but personable
